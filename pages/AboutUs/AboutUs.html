Our Mission
We empower every pet’s lifelong health journey by fueling a new era of wellness with a continuously evolving health roadmap — using AI to anticipate problems, personalize care, and strengthen the bond between pets and the people who love them.
CompanAIn at its Core
A team of specialized AI agents built to support every step of your pet’s health journey — from lab results to long-term care.
Modeled after Real Vet Teams
Just like veterinary clinics rely on specialists, our system is built from agents with dedicated expertise — no one-size-fits-all logic here.
Expert Agents, Focused Roles
Each agent specializes in tasks like lab interpretation, clinical triage, and long-term health analysis, ensuring deep, domain-specific intelligence.
Insights You Can Trust
This approach makes insights more context-aware, trustworthy, and aligned with veterinary logic — with the wellbeing of animals and their people always at the heart.
Real Impact
The result: better-informed vets, more reliable guidance for pet owners, and care that’s personalized, compassionate, and smart.
